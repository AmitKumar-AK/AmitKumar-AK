## 👨‍💼 About Me

> *"By helping others, you will learn how to help yourselves."* 🤝

I'm **Amit Kumar** - a **Solution Architect** with **21+ years** in the Microsoft ecosystem, specialising in building scalable, secure **Digital Experience Platforms (DXPs)**. I architect composable, headless, and cloud-native solutions at the intersection of **SitecoreAI**, **.NET**, **Azure**, and **Agentic AI / MCP servers**.

- 🏆 **8× Sitecore Technology MVP** (2019 - 2026) - recognised for consistent community impact and platform expertise
- 🤖 Building with **MCP Servers**, **GenAI**, and **Agentic AI** architectures on Azure & Sitecore
- 💻 Currently deep-diving into **SitecoreAI (XM Cloud)**, **.NET**, **Next.js**, and **GitHub Copilot** integrations
- ☁️ Exploring **Microsoft Azure**, **AWS**, and **Google Cloud** multi-cloud patterns
- 🌱 Passionate about **public speaking**, community knowledge-sharing, and open-source
- 🚀 Always ready to **collaborate** on dev experiments and architecture challenges
